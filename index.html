About

Dr. Karunakar ReddyMBBS, DNB(Ortho), MNAMS, FIJR

He is one of the young and dynamic leading Orthopedic Surgeon in Hyderabad. Experience spanning over 10 years in Orthopedic and Joint Replacement surgeries. He performed over 2000+ Orthopedic and Joint Replacement surgeries.

He completed his postgraduation at the Prestigious Ganga Institute and worked with orthopedic pioneers like Dr. Rajasekharan, Dr. Ajoy Shetty, Dr. Dheenadhayalan, Dr. Dhanasekara Raja, Dr. J.K Reddy, and many of eminent national faculty.

He specializes in Primary Knee, Hip Replacement Surgeries, Revision, and Complex Knee and Hip Replacement Surgeries, Arthroscopy, Sports Injuries, Complex Traumatic Fracture Fixation, and Pediatric Orthopedics, Robotic Replacement Surgeries.

He is a member of many national and international orthopedic associations like IOA, TOSA, IJO. He is currently working as a chief consultant and replacement surgeon at SRIKARA HOSPITALS, Pheerzadiguda branch.
